Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 March 2026

TripTracker Notes is a travel planning app that stores all data locally on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No accounts or sign-ups required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No data stored on external servers
  • No cookies

Local Storage

All trip data (checklists, transport, accommodation, notes, expenses) is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data is never uploaded to any server.

AI Features

When you use the AI Planner or Voice Input features, the app sends your trip details (destination, dates, checklist items) to Google Gemini via a secure proxy server to generate an itinerary or process voice input. This data is used solely to fulfil your request and is not stored on our servers. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed by Gemini.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is Google Gemini (via a Cloudflare Worker proxy) for AI-powered features. No other third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools are integrated.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children. Since no data is collected from any user, this policy applies equally to all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
