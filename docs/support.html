Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your trip data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

Do I need an account?

No. TripTracker Notes works entirely without an account or sign-up.

Does the AI Planner require internet?

Yes. The AI-powered itinerary generator needs an internet connection to work. All other features work offline.

Can I switch between English and Chinese?

Yes. Tap the language toggle in the app to switch anytime. Your preference is saved automatically.
